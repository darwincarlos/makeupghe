About me

Geraldine Carlos is a Marketing Graduate by profession. After spending 6 years in the corporate world, she got her interest in Makeup when a friend approached her to do some makeup in a pageant. Decided to pursue it and became her passion. She graduated in one of the top Makeup schools in the Philippines, the Center for Aesthetic Studies (CAS). Her passion lead her to work and was trained as a Professional Makeup Artist in one of the prestigious cosmetic brands such as LAURA MERCIER, THE MAKEUP STORE and MAC Cosmetics. With her experience and achievements, she is now currently working in REVLON Cosmetics as Brand Trainer. She conducts various trainings and seminars on their products to different places in the Philippines.

She works in all genres of make-up including the ultra-natural, the sophisticated, and the glamorous, yet she approaches each make-up as a unique study of the face, accentuating the natural qualities of each individual, while masking imperfections. Clients especially appreciate her soft, natural base, creating a look of freshness and light, and her original technique.
